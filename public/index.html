FAQ

Fair questions.

Is it really free?

Yes. No account, no trial, no watermark, no "pro" tier. It is an app made for friends.

Is it recording me all the time?

The buffer lives in memory and is thrown away as it rolls. Nothing touches your disk until you press the key. Close it from the tray and it is fully off.

Does anything get uploaded?

No. Klips stay on your PC. Even voice control runs offline with a local model, so "Kaleb Klipper, catch that" never leaves your room.

Windows says it protected my PC?

That is SmartScreen being careful with apps it has not seen before. Click More info, then Run anyway. The app is not signed with a paid certificate yet, that is all it means.

Where do my klips go?

Videos\Kaleb Klipper by default, as normal MP4 files you can move anywhere. The folder and the storage cap are yours to change in Settings.

How do updates work?

The app checks quietly in the background. A ready update installs on the next shutdown, or immediately if you click Restart & update now on the toast.
