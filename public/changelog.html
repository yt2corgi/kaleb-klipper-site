Changelog

What changed, and when.

The newest version is always the one the download button gives you. Older installs update themselves.

1.10.xThe app that updates itself
  • Auto updates: the app checks in the background and installs new versions on shutdown, or instantly from the restart toast
  • This website, with a download that always points at the newest build
  • Redesigned notifications: icon tile, countdown bar, and they stay put while your mouse is over them
  • Black outlines removed from every button, chip, keycap, toggle and dropdown
1.9.xGradients everywhere
  • Two-color gradient themes with an angle dial
  • Live preview: the whole app wears your theme while you mix it
  • Gradients now paint button edges and shadows too, not just the fill
  • Trim moved onto the playback timeline, with filmstrip frames and a waveform
  • New playhead, library zoom with Ctrl + and Ctrl -, ten stock klip sounds
  • Rounded corners as its own toggle, steadier starfield while resizing
1.8.xThe big library update
  • Favourites tab and starring, library search, klip dates
  • Hover a klip to preview it, no play button needed
  • Quality settings: 480p to 1080p, 30 or 60 fps
  • Monitor quick-switch hotkeys and an active klipping pause
  • Themes got their own tab, plus the glossy liquid glass look
  • Smooth per-element theme fade, animation fixes on picky PCs
1.6 to 1.7Make it yours
  • Custom themes with a hand-built color picker, editable in place
  • Custom MP3 klip sound and optional app sounds
  • Rename from the player, no more autoplay, drag a klip out to share
  • Startup fanfare and entrance animation, dismissable toasts
  • A practice range in Settings to test your klipping
1.3 to 1.5Hands-free era
  • Voice control: "Kaleb Klipper, catch that", fully offline
  • Voice open, close and shutdown commands with per-command toggles
  • Full shutdown from the titlebar power button
  • A custom-built video player to replace the browser default
1.0 to 1.2The foundation
  • Rolling replay buffer, one-key klips, MP4 library
  • Trim editor that always keeps your original safe
  • Mic mixing fixes, color themes, tray mode, onboarding
  • The first installer went out to the squad
